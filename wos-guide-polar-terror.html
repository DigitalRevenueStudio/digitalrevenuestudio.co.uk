Polar Terror:
Complete Hunting
Guide

Polar Terrors are the highest stamina-efficiency target in Whiteout Survival when hunted correctly. The right heroes, the right rally approach and the right timing turns them into one of the best F2P point sources in the game.

Beasts vs Polar Terrors

Which Is Better to Hunt?

🐻 Regular Beasts
Solo attack, 10 stamina each 1 point per beast. No alliance needed. Good for early game progression and stamina efficiency at lower levels. Level 26+ beasts give the best SvS points per stamina of any solo target.

Best for: SvS Beast Slay day (Level 26+ only), early game, solo play, when alliance is inactive.
🧊 Polar Terrors
Rally required, 20 to 25 stamina 3 points per kill. More stamina per hunt but better points-to-stamina ratio. Rewards include Hardened Alloy, resources and event points.

Best for: Beast Slay events, SvS Day 3 rally points, active alliance coordination sessions.

The efficiency calculation: With Gina at max skill (20 stamina per Polar Terror), 3 Polar Terrors cost 60 stamina for 9 points. 9 regular beasts at Level 26+ also cost around 72 stamina for 9 points. Polar Terrors win on efficiency but only when your alliance is actively calling and filling rallies.

Stamina Efficiency

The Gina Advantage

Gina is the single most important hero for hunting. Her Endurance Training skill reduces stamina costs and her Quick Paced skill increases march speed, meaning faster attacks and more hunts per session.

Rally Strategy

How to Hunt as a Team

Enable Auto-Join rallies

This is the single most important setting for Polar Terror hunting. Auto-Join means your troops participate in alliance rallies automatically even while you are managing other things. Enable it before every Beast Slay event.

Only target Polar Terrors your alliance can reliably kill

A failed rally wastes all participants' stamina with zero rewards. Coordinate with R4/R5 on which tier of Polar Terror your alliance can consistently defeat at current power levels before anyone opens a rally.

Coordinate timing with alliance members

Rallies that go unfilled expire and waste the caller's stamina. Communicate in alliance chat before opening a Polar Terror rally to confirm enough members are available to join. Peak session times get the most fills.

Lead rallies rather than just joining them

On SvS Day 3, rally leadership scores points separately from joining. If your alliance is active and filling rallies reliably, leading your own rallies on top of joining others maximises your session total.

Event Timing

When Polar Terrors Matter Most

Polar Terrors are worth hunting throughout the week but their value spikes significantly during specific event windows:

Beast Slay event (2 days): The primary Polar Terror farming window. 3 points per kill scored directly. Run all your stamina here. If your alliance coordinates well, this is the highest single-event scoring opportunity for F2P players outside of SvS.

SvS Prep Day 3: Beast and rally kills score heavily during the SvS Beast Hunt day. Polar Terrors give 3 points versus 1 for standard beasts. Save all stamina for this day specifically. Do not start beast rallies on Day 2 as covered in the SvS Prep guide.

Outside events: Polar Terror hunting still provides good resources (Hardened Alloy) outside events. It is worth maintaining the habit but not worth burning stamina stockpiles casually when a Beast Slay or SvS window is approaching.

Common Mistakes

What Most Players Get Wrong

Not enabling Auto-Join before Beast Slay events. Players who miss Auto-Join miss every rally that fires while they are not actively watching the screen. Enable it at the start of every Beast Slay event without exception.

Joining rallies that cannot kill the target. A failed Polar Terror hunt wastes all participants' stamina with no reward. If your alliance consistently fails on a specific Polar Terror tier, drop to a lower tier until power levels improve.

Hunting without Gina active in your formation. Gina reduces stamina cost per rally significantly. Always confirm Gina is in your exploration formation before starting a hunting session. Hunting without her active is a consistent stamina drain across every session.

Burning stamina casually before Beast Slay or SvS Day 3. Stamina spent on low-level beasts the day before a Beast Slay event is stamina that is not available during the scoring window. Save your full stamina pool specifically for event windows.
