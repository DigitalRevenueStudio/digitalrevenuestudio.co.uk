Council Tax Band Challenge Letter

Use this template to formally challenge your council tax band with the Valuation Office Agency. Free to use — no email required.

How to use this template

  1. Replace every highlighted placeholder with your own details.
  2. Research comparable properties on your street using the gov.uk council tax band search.
  3. Find 1991 sale prices for comparable properties using Zoopla historical data where available.
  4. Send the completed letter to the Valuation Office Agency via gov.uk or by post.
  5. Keep a copy for your records and note the date you submitted.

I am writing to formally challenge the council tax band currently assigned to my property at the above address. I believe the property has been placed in an incorrect band and I am requesting a review under the Council Tax (Alteration of Lists and Appeals) (England) Regulations 2009.

Current band: [e.g. Band D]
Band I believe to be correct: [e.g. Band C]
Date I became liable for council tax at this property: [Date you moved in]

Comparable property 1:
Address: [Address of comparable property]
Current band: [Band]
Description: [Brief description — e.g. "Semi-detached, 3 bed, same street, similar size and age"]

Comparable property 2:
Address: [Address of comparable property]
Current band: [Band]
Description: [Brief description]

Comparable property 3 (if available):
Address: [Address of comparable property]
Current band: [Band]
Description: [Brief description]

Based on the above, I believe my property should be placed in [Band] rather than its current [Band]. The comparable properties are of similar size, type and age to my own, and are situated in the same immediate area.

[Optional — add any additional evidence here, such as 1991 sale prices if you have found them]

Requested outcome

I am requesting that the VOA reviews the banding of my property and amends it accordingly. If a reduction is confirmed, I would also ask that any overpayment be calculated and refunded in full, backdated to the date I became liable for council tax at this address.

I am happy to provide any further information required. Please respond in writing to the address above or by email to [Your Email Address].

Yours faithfully,

[Your Full Name]
