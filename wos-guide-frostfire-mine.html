Frostfire Mine:
The Strategy
Most Players Miss

Most players head straight for the veins and the Smelter. There is a better approach: one that turns patrol guards into your primary orichalcum engine without ever needing to contest the most crowded part of the map.

Events Guide ✓ Updated April 2026

Core Strategy
Farm patrol guards in the outskirts. Skip the Smelter.
Skill Priority
Unlock Skills 1, 2, 3 as fast as possible

Most players approach Frostfire Mine the same way: rush to the highest-level veins, fight over the Smelter, gather until time runs out. It works, but it is not the highest-scoring approach available. There is a better strategy and it does not require contesting the Smelter at all.

The Core Idea

Patrol Guards Are Your Orichalcum Engine

The stated goal is to collect the highest amount of orichalcum. The two obvious methods are gathering veins and occupying the Smelter. But there is a third lever most players ignore entirely: patrol guard kills.

Defeating guards earns HP, which fills a progress bar at the bottom left of your screen. That bar unlocks skills, and the right skills transform every subsequent guard kill and vein occupation into a direct orichalcum payout. The entire strategy is built around unlocking those skills as fast as possible.

Where to Play

Stay in the Outskirts

Resist the instinct to head for the centre. The outskirts offer lower player density and a steady patrol guard supply. More guards available, less competition for veins, lower risk of disruption. This is where the strategy operates.

Use the free Advanced Teleports. Frostfire Mine provides them inside the battlefield. If your area runs dry of patrol guards, teleport to a fresh outskirt zone immediately. Time spent waiting for respawns is orichalcum lost.

First Priority

Kill Guards First: No Veins Yet

When the event starts, send all your marches to patrol guards. Do not gather any veins yet. Early vein gathering yields almost nothing compared to what your skills unlock. Focus entirely on guard kills until Skill 3 is active.

Skill Order

The Correct Skill Sequence

Follow this exact order. Getting it wrong costs you a significant portion of your total orichalcum for the session.

1
1,500 Orichalcum per Guard Kill Right Branch
Every patrol guard you defeat now pays out directly. The most important skill in the event. Unlock this first, every time.
2
March Speed +25% Right Branch
Faster marches means more guards killed per hour. Compounds Skill 1 immediately. Unlock second.
3
5,000 Orichalcum per Vein Occupation Left Branch
Triggers once per minute when you occupy any vein. This activates the vein cycling loop: occupy, collect, recall, repeat. Unlock third.
4
Gathering Speed +15% Left Branch
Boosts orichalcum yield from any vein your marches hold. Unlock fourth.
5
Gathering Speed +66% Left Branch: Always Take This
Always take the left branch for Skill 5. The right branch version requires holding veins for 5 continuous minutes, which cancels your Skill 3 vein loop entirely.

Never take Right branch Skill 5. It directly conflicts with the Skill 3 vein cycle and will reduce your total orichalcum for the session. Left branch only, every time.

Running the Event

Phase by Phase Execution

Phase 1: Start to Skill 3 Unlocked
All marches on patrol guards. No veins. Watch the HP bar at the bottom left. Unlock Skills 1, 2, then 3 in order. Do not touch any veins during this phase.
Phase 2: Skill 3 Active
Add the vein loop: one march occupies a vein every 60 seconds, collects the 5,000 orichalcum trigger, recalls, repeats. There is no visual notification. Track your 60-second cycle using the event timer at the top of the screen. All other marches stay on patrol guards. Unlock Skill 4 during this phase.
Phase 3: Skill 5 Active
Same rhythm as Phase 2. The +66% gathering boost now compounds your vein yield. Teleport to fresh outskirt zones when guards thin out in your current area.
Vein Outburst Windows
Stop guards immediately when a Vein Outburst triggers. All marches to outburst nodes. Hold for the full 5 minutes. Do not recall early during outbursts. Then return to patrol guards.
Final Minutes
Back to guard kills until the last second. Every kill is 1,500 orichalcum. Do not stop early.
Quick Tips

Things to Remember

  • Teleport freely between outskirt zones. Do not wait for guards to respawn in a depleted area.
  • Reserve exactly one march for the 60-second vein loop at all times. No more than one.
  • Watch nearby occupied veins. Claim them the moment they are vacated by another player.
  • During outbursts: activate the Skill 5 trigger before sending your troops, not after.
  • Kill guards until the absolute final second. The clock is still paying out orichalcum.
Common Mistakes

What Most Players Get Wrong

Rushing to the Smelter or high-level veins at the start. The Smelter is fiercely contested. Players who rush there often lose marches early and miss the Phase 1 guard kill window that unlocks the entire strategy. Stay in the outskirts at the start.

Gathering veins before Skill 3 is unlocked. Early vein gathering before Skill 3 is active provides almost no orichalcum. Every march sent to a vein during Phase 1 is a march not killing guards, which delays skill unlocking and shrinks your total yield.

Taking Right branch Skill 5. This is the single most damaging skill mistake in the event. Right branch Skill 5 requires 5 minutes of continuous vein occupation which directly cancels the 60-second Skill 3 vein cycle. Always take Left branch.

Leaving marches on veins for more than 60 seconds during normal phases. The Skill 3 payout triggers once per minute. Keeping a march on a vein for 3 minutes gives the same trigger count as cycling it every 60 seconds, but wastes march capacity that could be killing guards.

Stopping guard kills early in the final minutes. Many players switch entirely to vein gathering for the last few minutes. Guard kills continue paying 1,500 orichalcum each until the event clock hits zero. Keep marches on guards right until the end.
