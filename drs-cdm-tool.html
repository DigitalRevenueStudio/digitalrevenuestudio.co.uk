What it does

Everything you need for CDM compliance. Nothing you don't.

Pre-Start / Mobilisation Phase
F10 notifications, SHE plan status, designer appointments, welfare provisions — all tracked in one place.
Construction Phase
Site establishment, supervision checks, inductions, subcontractor management and ongoing compliance items.
Running Action Log
Track open actions with owners, due dates and status. Overdue items flagged automatically. Full history retained.
Photo Evidence
Attach photo evidence directly to checklist items. Embedded in the PDF export for a complete audit trail.
Monthly Carry-Forward
Save a snapshot at month end and carry forward open items automatically. No data lost between reporting periods.
One-Click PDF Export
Generate a professional compliance report instantly. Share with clients, principal designers or auditors without reformatting anything.
Works Offline
Browser-based HTML file. Open it on site, in a cabin, on a tablet. No internet connection required once downloaded.
Any Device
Phone, tablet or desktop. No app to install, no software licence, no IT approval needed.
