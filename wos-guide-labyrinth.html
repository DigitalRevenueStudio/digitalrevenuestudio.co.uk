The Labyrinth:
Complete F2P
Strategy Guide

The Labyrinth unlocks at Furnace Level 19 and runs on a weekly reset cycle. Most players underperform here because they use the wrong troop ratios and hero setup for each zone. This guide covers everything: zone schedule, how each zone works, troop ratios, squad strategy and shop priorities.

How It Works

The Labyrinth Explained

🔓
How to Unlock

Requires Furnace Level 19 and a Level 1 Command Center. On new servers it typically opens around week 2. Some zones have additional Furnace level requirements to access.

⚔
Combat System

Uses Expedition stats and skills, not Exploration stats. Think PvE combat like Beast or Mercenary fights, not Arena. Your Expedition hero setup determines your performance here, not your Exploration power.

🔄
Weekly Reset

Stage progress resets every Monday when the underground world fills with mist. After Monday reset you can re-challenge cleared stages for Raid Rewards. The Raid instant-clear feature unlocks after clearing Stage 10.

⏳
Daily Attempts

5 challenge attempts per zone daily, refreshing at 00:00 UTC. Use all 5. Failing attempts is how you find the right ratio for your account. Missing them permanently reduces your weekly token accumulation.

🧱
Troops Provided

Monday to Saturday zones use Level 10 troops provided by the Labyrinth. No troop loss risk. Sunday's Gaia Heart zone uses your own troops but you will not suffer casualties or wounded troops there either.

🏆
Best Rewards

Prioritise Mithril and Fire Crystal Shards from the Labyrinth shop. These are the highest long-term value items available and are hard to source elsewhere as F2P.

Zone Schedule

Which Zone Opens Each Day

Mon & Tue
Land of the Brave
Stat source: Heroes, Hero Gear, Exclusive Gear. Focus on hero Expedition skills and gear quality for these two days.
Wed & Thu
Cave of Monsters / Glowstone Mine
Cave stat source: Pets and Pet Skills. Glowstone stat source: Chief Charms. Glowstone Mine requires Furnace 25 to access.
Fri & Sat
Earthlab / Dark Forge
Stat source: Research Center and War Academy tech. Dark Forge requires Furnace 22 with Chief Gear equipped.
Sunday
Gaia Heart
Uses your own troops (no loss risk). Requires 3 squads. Higher-tier Fire Crystal and Helios troops have a real advantage here.

Key principle: Adjust your gear and hero setup based on which zone is open that day. The stat source changes daily and the same formation will not perform equally across all zones. F2P players should maintain separate saved formations for each zone type.

Troop Ratios

The Right Ratio for Each Zone

Getting the troop ratio right for each zone is the single most impactful thing you can do to improve your Labyrinth score. The zones favour different troop compositions based on their combat mechanics.

Gaia Heart: Sunday Strategy

The Three-Squad Approach

Gaia Heart is Sunday's zone and the only one that uses your own troops. It requires three squads deployed simultaneously. The approach that works best for F2P:

Squad 1: Main DPS

Your strongest hero formation with your highest-tier troops. This squad carries the bulk of the damage. Use your best Expedition skill heroes here regardless of troop type.

Squad 2: Support

A balanced formation with a healer hero if available (Philly). This squad's job is survival and sustain rather than raw damage output.

Squad 3: Buffer

Your third strongest available formation. Even a weaker third squad contributes to total damage and can trigger combo mechanics that boost Squad 1 output.

Common Mistakes

What Most Players Get Wrong

Using the same formation for every zone. The stat source changes daily. A formation optimised for Land of the Brave (hero-based) will underperform significantly in Glowstone Mine (Chief Charm based). Set up and save separate formations for each zone type.

Missing daily attempt windows. Labyrinth attempts refresh daily and cannot be banked. Missing a day permanently reduces your weekly token total. Treat it as a daily habit, not a weekly task.

Spending tokens on food, wood or coal. The Labyrinth shop has some of the best Mithril and Fire Crystal availability in the game. Spending tokens on basic resources that you can gather passively is one of the most common shop mistakes.

Not adjusting hero gear between zones. The gear you wear matters as much as the troops you bring. Swap to gear that boosts the active stat source each day: hero gear for Land of the Brave, pet-boosted gear for Cave of Monsters, research-boosted gear for Earthlab and Dark Forge.
