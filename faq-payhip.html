What Is Payhip?

Payhip is an e-commerce platform built for digital products. You upload files, set a price, and Payhip handles the checkout, payment processing and automatic file delivery to the buyer. There is nothing technical to manage. The free plan is genuinely functional for a new seller.

We use it as the storefront for our our studio work digital products — including a ChatGPT prompt workbook and the Whiteout Survival strategy guide. It is the platform we reviewed and currently recommend for beginners.

When does upgrading make sense? At 5% on the free plan, you break even on the Plus upgrade at approximately £580/month in sales. Below that, the free plan costs you less overall. Start free, upgrade when the maths justifies it.

Are there any other fees on top of the transaction fee?

Payhip passes through Stripe or PayPal payment processing fees, which are separate from Payhip's own transaction fee. Stripe typically charges around 1.4% + 25p for European cards and 2.9% + 25p for non-European cards. These are standard payment processing costs not specific to Payhip — any platform using Stripe or PayPal will have the same underlying fees.

Do I need a business account or company registration to sell on Payhip?

No. You can sell as an individual. You will need a payment account (Stripe or PayPal) to receive funds, but neither requires you to be a registered company. Be aware of your tax obligations — income from digital product sales is taxable in the UK, and you should keep records of earnings regardless of whether you are formally registered as self-employed.

What Can You Sell?

What types of digital products does Payhip support?

Payhip supports: downloadable files (PDFs, ebooks, templates, spreadsheets, images, software), online courses, memberships, coaching products and physical products. For most digital content creators the download and course options are the most relevant.

Is there a file size limit for uploads?

Yes — the maximum file size for a single upload is 5GB. For most digital products (PDFs, ebooks, templates, guides) this limit is never a constraint. Larger software files or video-heavy course content may need to consider this.

Can I sell the same product for different prices — including free?

Yes. Payhip supports pay-what-you-want pricing, fixed pricing and free downloads. Free products are a useful lead-generation tool — you can offer a free guide, collect the buyer's email, and follow up through a separate email platform. We use this approach to distribute our free DRS guide.

Traffic and Discovery

This is the part most Payhip coverage glosses over. Payhip is not a marketplace — it has no internal search or browse function for buyers to discover your products. Customers arrive only if you bring them. There is no Amazon-equivalent organic discovery on Payhip.

“The platform handles the transaction flawlessly. Getting someone to the checkout — that is entirely on you. Payhip cannot substitute for an audience.”

Does Payhip have an affiliate programme?

Yes — two separate ones. Payhip has a referral programme where existing users can refer new sellers and earn a small ongoing commission. They also allow you to set up your own affiliate programme for your products, so other people can promote your products for a share of the revenue. Both are available on the free plan.
