Whiteout Survival Guide —
From one Reddit post to 323,000 views

A niche gaming resource built entirely on free tools. Blog, wiki, infographics, gift code tracking and a growing Reddit community — built from a single post and genuine community engagement.

The brief

Community first. Content second.

The WOS Guide did not start with a content plan. It started with a Reddit post that got 17,000 views, 56 upvotes and 59 comments overnight. The community told us what they needed — and we built it.

One comment in particular shaped the entire strategy: "there are hundreds of guides like this online, just not put into a single wiki." Three words changed the build plan. The WOS Wiki was the direct result of listening to the community rather than assuming what they wanted.

“There are hundreds of guides like this online — just not in a single wiki.” — ChandlerOG, r/whiteoutsurvival

The entire brand is built on a free-first principle. Every guide, every wiki page, every gift code — available without a login, without a paywall, without a subscription. Trust first. Revenue follows.
