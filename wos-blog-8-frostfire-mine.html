Frostfire Mine
The Strategy
Most Players Miss

Most players head straight for the veins and the Smelter. There is a better approach — one that turns patrol guards into your primary orichalcum engine without ever needing to contest the most crowded part of the map.

WOS Guide Frostfire Mine F2P Strategy March 2026

Most players approach Frostfire Mine the same way — rush to the highest-level veins, fight over the Smelter, gather until time runs out. It works, but it is not the highest-scoring approach available to you. There is a better strategy, and it does not require contesting the Smelter at all.

The Core Idea

Patrol Guards Are Your Orichalcum Engine

The stated goal is to collect the highest amount of orichalcum. The two obvious methods are gathering veins and occupying the Smelter. But there is a third lever most players ignore entirely: patrol guard kills.

Defeating guards earns HP, which fills a progress bar at the bottom left of your screen. That bar unlocks skills — and the right skills transform every subsequent guard kill and vein occupation into a direct orichalcum payout. The entire strategy is built around unlocking those skills as fast as possible.

Where to Play

Stay in the Outskirts

Resist the instinct to head for the centre. The outskirts — lower player density, steady patrol guard supply — are where this strategy operates. More guards available, less competition for veins, lower risk of disruption.

Use the free Advanced Teleport. Frostfire Mine provides them inside the battlefield. If your area runs dry of patrol guards, teleport to a fresh outskirt zone immediately. Time spent waiting is orichalcum lost.

Priority Rule

Kill Guards First — No Veins Yet

When the event starts, send all your marches to patrol guards. Do not gather any veins. This feels counterintuitive but it is critical — early vein gathering yields almost nothing compared to what your skills will unlock. Focus entirely on guard kills until Skill 3 is active.

Skill Order

The Correct Skill Sequence

This is where most players go wrong. Follow this exact order — choosing incorrectly can cost you your ranking.

1
1,500 Orichalcum per Guard Kill Right Branch
Every patrol guard you defeat now pays out directly. The most important skill in the event — unlock this first, every time.
2
March Speed +25% Right Branch
Faster marches = more guards killed per hour. Compounds Skill 1 immediately. Unlock second.
3
5,000 Orichalcum per Vein Occupation Left Branch
Triggers once per minute when you occupy a vein. This activates the vein cycling loop — occupy, collect, recall, repeat. Unlock third.
4
Gathering Speed +15% Left Branch
Boosts orichalcum yield from any vein your marches hold. Unlock fourth.
5
Gathering Speed +66% Left Branch — Take This One
Always take the left branch Skill 5. The right branch version requires holding veins for 5 continuous minutes — it cancels your Skill 3 vein loop entirely.

Never take Right Branch Skill 5. It conflicts with your Skill 3 vein cycle and reduces your total orichalcum. Left branch only.

Running the Event

The Event Phase by Phase

Phase 1 — Start to Skill 3

All marches on patrol guards. No veins. Watch the HP bar at the bottom left. Unlock Skills 1, 2, then 3 in order.

Phase 2 — Skill 3 Active

Add the vein loop: one march occupies a vein every 60 seconds, collects the 5,000 orichalcum trigger, recalls, repeats. No visual notification — track your 60-second cycle using the event timer at the top of the screen. All other marches stay on guards. Unlock Skill 4.

Phase 3 — Skill 5 Active

Same rhythm. The +66% gathering boost now compounds your vein yield. Teleport to fresh outskirt zones when guards thin out.

Vein Outburst Windows

Stop guards immediately. All marches to vein outburst nodes. Hold for the full 5 minutes — do not recall early. Then return to patrol guards.

Final Minutes

Back to guard kills until the last second. Every kill is 1,500 orichalcum. Do not stop early.

Quick Tips

Things to Remember

  • Teleport freely between outskirt zones — do not wait for guards to respawn
  • Reserve exactly one march for the vein loop at all times — no more
  • Watch nearby occupied veins — claim them the moment they are vacated
  • During outbursts: activate Skill 5 trigger before sending your troops, not after
  • Kill guards until the absolute final second — the clock is still paying out
