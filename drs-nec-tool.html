Features

Everything the NEC process actually requires.

Early Warning Notice Register
Log EWNs under NEC4 Cl.15.1 / NEC3 Cl.16.1. Track status from Open through Meeting Arranged to Closed. Record resolution and escalation to CE where applicable.
Full CE Assessment Cycle
All four stages in one record — Notification, Quotation, PM Response and Agreement / Determination. Time and cost impact tracked at each stage.
NEC Time Bar Warnings
Flags when NEC deadlines are approaching — PM 2-week response window, contractor 8-week notification limit, quotation submission deadlines.
Clause 60.1 Sub-Clause Reference
All 18 Cl.60.1 sub-clauses available as a dropdown on every CE record. Select the contractual basis and record the entitlement narrative.
PDF Export — Issue-Ready Notices
Generate a professional EWN notice or full CE assessment record in one click. Formatted and ready to issue to the Project Manager or Employer’s representative.
Works Offline on Any Device
Browser-based HTML files. Open in Chrome, Safari or Edge on phone, tablet or desktop. No app, no software licence, no IT approval needed. Works in a site cabin with no signal.
