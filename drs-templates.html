Council Tax

Council Tax Band Challenge Letter

Use this to formally request a review of your council tax band from the Valuation Office Agency. Works best when you have identified comparable properties nearby in a lower band.

Send to the VOA Free to submit No solicitor needed

To: Valuation Office Agency

Re: Request to review council tax band — [Your full address and postcode]

Dear Sir or Madam,

I am writing to formally request a review of the council tax band assigned to my property, currently listed as Band [Your current band, e.g. D].

Having reviewed the VOA’s publicly available records, I have identified the following comparable properties which are assigned lower bands despite being of similar size, type and construction:

[Property 1: full address — Band X]
[Property 2: full address — Band X]
[Property 3: full address — Band X]

I believe the current banding of my property is incorrect and respectfully request that it be reviewed and amended accordingly.

Please confirm receipt and advise on next steps.

Yours faithfully,
[Your full name]
[Your address]
[Date]
