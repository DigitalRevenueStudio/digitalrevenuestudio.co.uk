Hall of Chiefs
Complete F2P
Event Guide

Hall of Chiefs is one of the most important early events in WOS — it runs 3 times in Generation 1 and rewards you for doing the right things in the right order. Most players leave significant points on the table by spending on the wrong day. This guide covers the correct strategy day by day.

WOS Guide Hall of Chiefs Gen 1 & Gen 2 March 2026
What Is Hall of Chiefs

The Event Context

When It Runs

Exclusive to Generation 1 and Generation 2 states. Runs 3 times in Gen 1 (6 days each). In Gen 2 it runs 7 days and the featured hero switches from Jeronimo to Philly. After Gen 2 it is replaced by King of Icefield.

Why It Matters

The top 10 players overall win Jeronimo shards — the best Gen 1 combat hero. Even outside the top 10, milestone rewards include Gems, Speedups, Mythic Skill Manuals and resources that meaningfully accelerate your progression.

The Core Rule

Each day has a specific scoring category. Spending resources on the wrong day scores nothing for HoC — you just lose the resource. Hoard everything in advance and spend it only on the day that rewards it.

F2P Reality Check

Top 10 overall requires significant spending. Do not chase it as F2P. Focus on hitting all 4 daily milestone point thresholds every day — those rewards compound and are fully achievable without spending real money.

The single most important HoC habit: Wait for President skills to activate before spending anything each day. President skills boost the points value of every action — spending before they activate wastes the multiplier on resources you cannot recover.

The 6-Day Schedule

Day by Day — What to Do When

1
Construction, Research & Training
Focus: CONSTRUCTION — highest points here today
Do Today
  • Construction Speedups — primary focus. Use all saved building speedups here.
  • Fire Crystals — use them today, they score on this day's category.
  • Chief Charm Upgrades — contribute to power gain scoring.
Save for Later
  • Research Speedups — save for Day 4. Research scores more points on Day 4 than Day 1.
  • Training Speedups — save for Day 3 and 4.
  • Pet Upgrades, Gear, Essence Stones — all save for Day 5 or 6.
👑 Wait for President skills before spending speedups or Fire Crystals.
2
Lucky Wheel, Hero Shards & Gathering
90,000 points per Lucky Wheel spin
Do Today
  • Spin the Lucky Wheel — 90,000 points per spin. Spend your Gems here to unlock the featured hero (Flint in Gen 1 later events) and earn shards.
  • Hero Shards — use them today. Point values: 4,000 / 14,000 / 35,000 per tier.
  • Gathering with Gathering Buff — activate gathering boost and send all marches.
Critical — Save These
  • Gold Hero Shards — save for the last day of HoC for maximum scoring efficiency.
  • Do NOT start beast rallies today — beast scoring is not active yet. Start Day 3.
  • Pet Upgrades, Gear materials — all save for Day 5/6.
3
Troop Training
Training higher-tier troops = significant point boost
Do Today
  • Training Speedups — use all saved training speedups. Train the highest-tier troops available to you.
  • Upgrade low-tier troops to higher tiers — upgrading also earns training points (the difference in power between tiers scores points).
Save for Later
  • Pet Upgrades, Essence Stones, Gear materials — Day 5 and 6.
  • Remaining Research Speedups — Day 4.
👑 Wait for President skills before using training speedups.
4
Gathering & Research
Send troops early — first gathering points go to who queues first after reset
Do Today
  • Send troops to gather immediately after the daily reset — first movers get the early points lead on Day 4. Use Gathering Boost (8h = 200 Gems, 24h = 600 Gems).
  • Research Speedups — primary research window. Use all saved research speedups here.
Save for Days 5 & 6
  • Pet Upgrades
  • Chief Gear Essence Stones and Widgets
  • Chief Gear upgrade materials
👑 Wait for President skills before using research speedups.
5
Chief Gear & Hero Gear
Essence Stones, Widgets and Gear upgrades
Do Today
  • Chief Gear Essence Stones — use all saved Essence Stones.
  • Hero Widgets — use today.
  • Chief Gear upgrade materials — use here.
  • Pet Upgrades — if available.
👑 Wait for President skills before using any upgrade materials.
6
Final Push — Everything Goes
Widest scoring window — use all remaining saved resources
Use Everything Remaining
  • Gold Hero Shards — now is the time. Saved from Day 2 for maximum scoring.
  • Any remaining Construction Speedups
  • Any remaining Training Speedups
  • Any remaining Gear or upgrade materials
Final Day Tips
  • Check your current milestone point thresholds — see how close you are to the next milestone and prioritise actions that close that gap
  • Use gathering boost one final time if resources allow
👑 Last chance for President skills — activate before spending anything.

The pre-event hoard: In the weeks before HoC, stop using speedups, hero shards and upgrade materials. Every speedup burned casually the week before HoC is a speedup that does not score points during the event. The players who rank highest as F2P are almost always the ones who saved the longest.
