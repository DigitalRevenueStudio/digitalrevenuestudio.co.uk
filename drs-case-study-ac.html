The brief

A consultancy with 25 years experience. Zero digital presence.

Atker Consultancy Services is a project management consultancy with 25 years of combined experience in construction, infrastructure and public sector operations. When we began working with AC, they had no logo, no website and no digital brand.

The brief was a full digital build from zero: brand identity, website and at least one digital product that demonstrated the consultancy's expertise. The CDM Digital Audit Tool was the answer to that third brief — built by practitioners, for practitioners.

“Created by Atker Consultancy. Distributed by Digital Revenue Studio. The origin story is the product.”

The CDM Digital Audit Tool is now sold via Payhip at £14.99 — a browser-based CDM 2015 compliance checklist that works offline on any device. No software, no subscription, no installation. The first digital product to convert on the DRS/AC platform.
