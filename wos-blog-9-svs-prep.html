SvS Prep Phase
The 5-Day
F2P Playbook

State vs State is the biggest event in Whiteout Survival. Most players treat the prep phase as a warm-up. The players who rank consistently well treat it as the event itself — because prep phase scoring is where a large portion of your individual rank is decided before battle even starts.

WOS Guide State vs State F2P Strategy March 2026

The core rule: The prep phase has daily scoring events that reward specific actions on specific days. Using your research speedups on Day 1 instead of Day 5 scores you nothing — it just wastes them in the wrong window. Hold resources. Use them on the right day.

Before It Starts

Matchmaking — Before Prep Phase Opens

Before the prep phase even opens, there is an opportunity to influence which state you face. Matchmaking occurs on Saturday and Sunday UTC. If the top players in your state coordinate a power reduction during this window — removing hero gear, resetting hero levels through Drill Camp — you can influence the match in your favour.

Individual actions have minimal effect. It only works as a coordinated effort across your state's top 100–150 players. Speak to your state leaders about organising this before the window opens. The full matchmaking guide — including exactly what the system weighs — is in the SvS appendix of the paid guide.

The 5-Day Schedule

Day by Day — What to Do When

1
Construction & Chief Charms
Building scoring event active
Do Today
  • Fire Crystals — primary use window
  • Construction Speedups — push building upgrades
  • Chief Charm Upgrades — contribute to construction scoring
Save for Later
  • Training Speedups — Day 4
  • Research Speedups — Day 5
Wait for President skills before spending speedups or fire crystals.
2
Wheel, Shards & Gathering
Hero upgrade & gathering scoring active
Do Today
  • Spin Lucky Wheel — scoring window open
  • Use Hero Shards — hero upgrade scoring active
  • Gather Resources — gathering scoring event
Critical — Do Not Do
  • Do NOT start beast rallies today. The 48-hour event means starting Day 2 loses you a reward window. Start Day 3, collect both payouts together.
  • Save all Stamina for Day 3 beast hunting
  • Save Pet Upgrades — Day 5 only
3
Beast Hunt Day
Highest points-per-action day of the entire prep phase
Do Today — All Stamina Here
  • Kill Level 26+ Beasts — 10 stamina = 12,000 points
  • Call Polar Terror Rallies — 25 stamina = 30,000 points
Important Rules
  • Do not kill lower-level beasts — fewer points per stamina
  • Lead rallies, don't just join them — joining does not score points
  • Save Pet Upgrades — Day 5 only
Also OK Today
  • Lucky Wheel — scoring still active
  • Hero Shards — still open
4
Troops & Hero Gear
Troop training scoring event active
Do Today
  • Training Speedups — train and promote troops
  • Chief Charm Upgrades — secondary window
Save for Day 5
  • Pet Upgrades, Essence Stones, Hero Widgets
  • Chief Gear upgrades
  • Research & remaining Construction Speedups
Wait for President skills before using training speedups.
5
Final Push — Everything Goes
Widest scoring window of the prep phase
Use Everything Saved
  • Chief Gear Upgrades
  • Research Speedups
  • Essence Stones & Hero Widgets
  • Remaining Construction Speedups
  • Pet Upgrades — everything held from Days 1–4
Wait for President skills before spending — the Day 5 volume makes this the most impactful buff of the entire prep phase.
