Experts System —
Who to Prioritise First

The Experts system introduces powerful NPC characters who provide permanent passive buffs to your city and combat performance. Unlocked through Tundra Trek and Frontier Trek, and upgraded via the Dawn Academy using Books of Knowledge. Knowing which Expert to prioritise first is one of the most common questions from mid-game players.

Overview

How the Experts System Works

Experts are unlocked through the Tundra Trek and Frontier Trek game modes and upgraded at the Dawn Academy building using Books of Knowledge. Each Expert specialises in a different area and provides permanent bonuses that stack across your entire account.

Each Expert also has an Affinity system — raising Affinity Level through specific actions further enhances that Expert's talent skills. Focus Affinity investment on your primary Expert for compounding long-term benefits.

First Expert: Cyrille is the first Expert available and provides general development bonuses. She is your starting point. Upgrade her before branching into specialist Experts.

All Six Experts

Expert Breakdown by Specialisation

Cyrille — General Development

Your first Expert. Provides general city development bonuses including construction and research improvements. Upgrade first as she unlocks the Expert system and provides broad benefits from day one.

Agnes — Gathering Efficiency

Increases gathering speed and resource collection rates. High value for F2P players who depend on consistent world map gathering to fuel upgrades and troop training.

Romulus — Combat Performance

Enhances troop combat stats directly. Strong for players who participate heavily in PvP events — Foundry Battle, Fortress Battle, and SvS combat phases.

Baldur — Resource Production

Improves base resource production rates from city buildings. Useful for passive resource generation but lower priority than Romulus or Agnes for active players.

Holger — Research and Construction

Reduces research and construction times. High value if you are in a heavy upgrade phase pushing toward new Furnace levels or unlocking new troop tiers.

Fabian — Foundry Battle Specialist

At max level provides 30% attack and 30% defence bonuses to all allied troops in Foundry Battle and Tundra Hellfire events. Extremely valuable for alliances that compete seriously in Foundry Battle. Lower priority until you are established in a competitive alliance.

F2P Priority Order: Cyrille first (unlock requirement) → Agnes (gathering fuels everything) → Holger (speeds development) → Romulus (combat) → Fabian (endgame event specialist)

F2P Strategy

Investing in Experts Efficiently

Books of Knowledge are the primary upgrade currency for Experts and are limited for F2P players. The principle is the same as hero investment — go deep on one Expert rather than spreading resources thin.

Agnes is arguably the highest-value Expert for F2P players outside of established alliances. Gathering efficiency directly increases your daily resource income which fuels every other system in the game: troops, research and buildings.

Once Agnes is well-developed, Holger's construction and research speed bonuses compound significantly over time — every percentage reduction applies to every future upgrade you make.

Community note: Expert priorities are heavily debated in the WOS community and depend on your play style and alliance activities. This represents general F2P best practice — experienced players in your alliance may have different recommendations based on your specific server meta.
