Experts System:
Complete F2P Guide

The Experts system unlocks in the mid game and provides permanent passive bonuses across your city, combat and event performance. Knowing which Expert to invest in first, which to skip and how Sigils work is one of the most debated topics in WOS. This guide covers all Experts including the second generation, with priority order and F2P strategy.

Experts System ✓ Community Reviewed — April 2026

Overview

How the Experts System Works

Experts are NPC characters who provide permanent passive bonuses across your city, combat and event performance. The feature launched in August 2025 and requires Furnace Level 25 to unlock. Experts are unlocked through two routes: Tundra Trek (the F2P path, longer grind but fully accessible) and Frontier Trek (the faster pay-to-play path). Once invited to your city, Experts are upgraded at the Dawn Academy using Books of Knowledge and Expert Sigils.

There are currently three generations of Experts. Costs grow exponentially as you level up, so planning your investment order before you start spending is important.

Upfront note: Expert priorities are one of the most debated topics in WOS. The right order genuinely depends on your playstyle, alliance activities and what events you participate in. This guide presents the most common F2P consensus as a starting framework, not a fixed rule. Ask experienced players in your alliance what they prioritise and why.

Generation 1 Experts

Unlocks at ~Day 150 (Furnace 25, Fire Crystal Lv. 5)

Agnes

Agnes reduces construction time (by up to 8 hours at higher levels), provides daily Seeker Chests and grants extra Intel missions. More Intels means more rewards and more widgets, which directly boost hero stats. Her stamina and construction time skills are lower value but she is considered easy to max and high priority for virtually all F2P players. She is generally the first Expert most players invest in heavily.

Cyrille

Cyrille is the Bear Huntress. She increases Bear Hunt damage significantly and adds rally capacity for Bear hunts. Her skills for Bear Hunt rewards and Essence Stones are the primary targets. Skills 2 and 3 are the priority. Her value compounds over time as Bear Hunt is a consistent source of Chief Gear materials. A common approach is to develop Cyrille alongside Agnes rather than leaving her entirely until later.

Holger

Holger focuses on Arena. He provides large Arena Shop discounts (up to 50% off) and combat attribute bonuses for your Heroes. Several experienced players rate Holger very highly because Mithril and Mythic gear are among the biggest mid-to-late game bottlenecks, and Holger is the most direct route to getting more of them. Skills 2 and 3 are the priority. Affinity Level 50 is considered a strong stopping point for F2P before costs become too high.

Romulus

Romulus provides general troop stat boosts and increases both global rally capacity and troop capacity. He also provides free daily troops at higher levels. He is the most expensive Expert to upgrade and provides the least return for casual F2P players. However, for rally leaders and R4/R5 players who regularly lead Foundry Battle, Fortress Battle or SvS rallies, his rally capacity increases are genuinely valuable. The F2P consensus is to leave him until all other Experts are well-developed.

F2P note: Romulus costs far more than other Experts relative to what you get. Skip him or deprioritise heavily unless you are a large spender or regularly leading rallies for your alliance.

Generation 2 Experts

Unlocks at ~Day 195 (Hero Generation 4)

Baldur

Baldur's primary value is generating Expert Sigils, which are needed to upgrade all your other Experts. He also boosts points and rewards in alliance events including Alliance Championship and Crazy Joe. For many F2P players, Baldur will be the only consistent source of Sigils. Skill 2 (extra Sigils per week — approximately 15 free Sigils weekly at a good level) is the priority and should be activated as quickly as possible. Save Sigils and Books of Knowledge ahead of his unlock so you can invest immediately.

Fabian

Fabian earns additional tokens from the Foundry Battle shop and has a skill that instantly heals a set number of troops during Foundry Battle, which removes the need to leave the battle for 12 minutes to heal. His other skills are mainly useful for rally leaders. If you participate regularly in Foundry Battle, he has meaningful value. If you do not, he can be deprioritised significantly. Level 60 gets most of his useful skills to a high level and is a reasonable stopping point before costs become excessive.

Valeria

Valeria boosts SvS points, provides State of Power Tokens (used to buy Fire Crystals and Shards) and increases SvS rally capacity. Her second skill occasionally offers Expert Sigils in her shop, which should always be bought when available. Her capacity and stat boost skills are generally considered low value for F2P. She is worth investing in once Gen 1 Experts are well-developed. Save Sigils and Books of Knowledge ahead of her unlock.

Generation 3 Experts

Newest Additions (2026)

Ronne

Ronne is a legendary mercenary added in March 2026. She specialises in the Tundra Trade Route event, providing attack and defence bonuses for truck raiding, enhanced cargo gathering and additional daily truck refreshes. She is considered a niche, event-specific Expert rather than a top priority. Her utility is limited to a monthly event, and the community consensus is that she is good for getting more resources from the truck event as F2P but should only be invested in after your Gen 1 and Gen 2 Experts are well-developed.

Kathy

Kathy is the Mine Leader, added in April 2026. She focuses on the Frostfire Mine event. As the newest Expert, detailed community consensus on her priority order is still forming. Given Frostfire Mine is a bi-weekly event and a consistent source of Orichalcum for gear crafting, she may have meaningful value for active players. Her priority relative to other Experts will become clearer as the community develops strategies around her.

Note on Gen 3 Experts: Ronne and Kathy are brand new and the community is still evaluating them. The unlock requirements for Gen 3 Experts are not yet confirmed. Check with your alliance and the WOS community for the latest on both before investing heavily.

F2P Strategy

Getting the Most from Limited Resources

Expert upgrade costs grow exponentially. Going deep on your priority Experts rather than spreading resources thin is more efficient than trying to level everyone evenly. Once Baldur is available, getting his Sigil skill active quickly matters because those Sigils fund upgrades across all your other Experts — the compounding benefit over several months is significant.

Save Sigils and Books of Knowledge ahead of Gen 2 Expert unlocks (Baldur and Valeria) so you can invest in them quickly on arrival. The same logic applies to Ronne and Kathy as Gen 3 Experts, though the community is still establishing priority for those two.

Community note: Expert priority is one of the most debated topics in WOS and the right answer depends on your playstyle and goals. The order above reflects common F2P consensus but your alliance's R4/R5 will have views based on your specific server meta. Ask them before committing significant Books of Knowledge to any Expert.
