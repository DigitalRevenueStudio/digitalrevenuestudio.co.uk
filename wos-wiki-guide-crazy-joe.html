How It Works

The Core Mechanics

Crazy Joe sends waves of bandits attacking every alliance member's city simultaneously. Your job is to defend your own city and reinforce your allies — both earn points. The event runs on Tuesday and Thursday during SvS/KoI/HoC weeks. Shields do not work during this event.

Points rule: Points are based on the percentage of attackers you defeat — 100% of a wave = maximum points. The city owner earns points regardless of whether their own troops or alliance reinforcements do the killing. Reinforcing allies earns points at roughly 50% of the rate of defending your own city, but it is still worth doing.

Troop Strategy

Send vs Keep Home

✓ Send Out
  • All Infantry — send to nearby allies first. Infantry fight first in turn order and get the most kills.
  • All Lancers — send once Infantry is depleted from your marches.
  • Use all available marches — every march active is more points.
  • Prioritise reinforcing online players — Joe targets active players in waves 7, 14 and 17 for bonus points.
✕ Keep at Home
  • All Marksman — keep every Marksman in your base. They rarely contribute kills in most waves and are wasted when sent out.
  • Your 3 strongest heroes — keep them home to defend. They use Expedition skills during city defense which significantly boost your defense.
  • Defence heroes like Patrick and Sergey should be your lead heroes when sending squads out — not your strongest attackers.

The zero defence strategy: Advanced players above Furnace 26 sometimes send out all Infantry and Lancers leaving only Marksman at home, relying entirely on alliance reinforcements to defend. This requires tight coordination and pre-arranged reinforcement agreements with online members. Only attempt this if your alliance has confirmed cover for you.

Hero Setup

Which Heroes to Use Where

🏠
Defending your city: Keep your 3 strongest heroes at home on defence. Heroes use their Expedition skills during city defence — this makes a significant difference in how many bandits your troops eliminate. Avoid leaving Growth heroes like Zinman on defence — they contribute almost nothing.
🦵
Sending out squads: Use defence heroes like Patrick and Sergey as your first hero in each squad. Their skills reduce incoming damage and keep your reinforcement troops alive longer — which translates directly to more kills and more points for both you and the city you're defending.
⭐
Expert tip: The Expert Baldur's 3rd skill provides extra event-related gains in defensive battles. If you have Baldur developed, make sure he is active during Crazy Joe — it is one of the hidden multipliers most players miss.

Before the Event

Preparation Checklist

💬
Coordinate with your alliance. Agree a start time, confirm who is covering HQ for waves 10 and 20, and make sure members know to be online for waves 7, 14 and 17.
📨
Send reinforcements before wave 1. Pre-sending troops to alliance members means they are already defending when the first wave hits. Do not wait — troops sent after wave 1 starts are late.
⚡
Activate buffs and pets. Any troop attack, defence or health boost you have should go up before the event. Even a small percentage difference compounds across 20 waves.
🎥
Do not teleport during the event. Teleporting cancels your current event participation for that wave segment — waves 1–9 or 11–18. Stay put.
🔁
Swap troops with allies. Coordinating a troop exchange with 2–3 alliance members before the event means your troops defend their cities while theirs defend yours. Both sides earn points from the same troops.
