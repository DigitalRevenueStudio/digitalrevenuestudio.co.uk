Where Is Maverick? — Amazon KDP
The brief

A character before a brand

Mavericks Adventures started as a question at the dinner table: could a real dog — Maverick, our cockapoo — become the central character of a children's book series? The answer was yes. The challenge was building a brand around a character rather than a product.

Most KDP publishing content treats the platform as a mechanism — upload, price, wait. The DRS approach was different: build the character as the primary asset, treat the book as the entry point to something larger, and document everything honestly.

“The character is the brand. The book is just the first thing you can buy.”

We built the brand infrastructure first — logo, website, social identity — then published the book. Audience building and book publishing happened in parallel from week one.
