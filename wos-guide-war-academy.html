War Academy —
Troop Research Guide

The War Academy is where you unlock and upgrade your troop tiers through research. Getting the research order right is one of the most impactful decisions you make in Whiteout Survival. The wrong order wastes weeks of research time and leaves you training troops that are far weaker than they should be.

Overview

How the War Academy Works

The War Academy houses your Military research tree. This is where you unlock higher troop tiers, improve troop combat stats, and increase your march capacity. Every upgrade you research here directly affects every battle you fight.

Research in the War Academy requires time, resources, and in some cases prerequisite research to be completed first. The tree is deep, so prioritising correctly is essential for F2P players who cannot speed through every branch simultaneously.

Core rule: Always prioritise unlocking the next troop tier above combat stat improvements. A T8 troop with average stats beats a T6 troop with excellent stats. Get to the highest tier you can unlock first, then return to improve stats.

Research Priority

What to Research First

Early Game (Furnace 1–15)

Focus entirely on unlocking the next troop tier. Do not branch into combat stat research until you have unlocked the highest tier available to your Furnace level. Every resource spent on stat research before reaching your tier ceiling is a wasted resource.

Mid Game (Furnace 16–25)

Once your troop tiers match your Furnace level, begin researching troop attack, defence, and health improvements for your primary troop type. If your hero lineup is Infantry-focused, prioritise Infantry combat stats first.

Late Game (Furnace 26+)

March capacity research becomes increasingly important as you participate in larger-scale events. A higher march cap means more troops contributing to each rally or defence. Prioritise this alongside combat stat improvements once core tiers are unlocked.

Alliance Research: The War Academy feeds into Alliance Technology as well. Contributing to alliance Battle tech nodes improves combat performance for your entire alliance. Check which nodes your leadership has highlighted and contribute to those first.

F2P Troop Strategy

Building Troops as F2P Player

F2P troop strategy is quality over quantity. You cannot match the raw troop numbers of heavy spenders, but you can ensure every troop you train is as strong as possible.

Always train the highest tier you have unlocked. Never fill queues with lower-tier troops just to keep training running. One T9 troop is worth significantly more than three T6 troops in combat.

Upgrade existing troops before training new ones. Promoting lower-tier troops to higher tiers is more resource-efficient than training from scratch.

Keep your Hospital upgraded. Hospital capacity determines how many troops survive as wounded rather than dying permanently. A full Hospital during combat means permanent troop losses. Always check before engaging.

Auto-Join is your most important passive setting. Enable Alliance rally Auto-Join in settings. You accumulate rally rewards, hero fragments, and keep your troops earning even while offline. Never leave this off.
