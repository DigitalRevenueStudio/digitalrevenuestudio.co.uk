What Is Sender?

Sender (sender.net) is an email marketing platform with a generous free tier. It handles subscriber management, email campaigns, automation sequences and embedded signup forms. We use it as the email backbone for Digital Revenue Studio and Whiteout Survival Guide — two entirely separate audiences managed through separate accounts.

We reviewed it formally as part of our platform testing work. The short version: it does what it promises at the free tier, it is well-designed for beginners, and the free plan limits are among the most competitive available.

Does Sender put its branding on my emails?

Yes, on the free plan there is a small "Sent with Sender" footer badge on outgoing emails. It is unobtrusive but visible. If you want to remove it, you need to upgrade to a paid plan. For most new list builders this is an acceptable trade-off given the generous limits elsewhere.

Can I run automation on the free plan?

Yes. This is one of Sender's strongest points. Automation — including welcome sequences, drip campaigns and trigger-based emails — is available on the free plan. Most competing platforms restrict automation to paid tiers. We use Sender's automation for the welcome email sequence on both DRS and WOS.

Signup Forms

How do I embed a Sender form on an external website?

In the Sender dashboard, go to Forms, create or select a form, then use the embed code provided. This is a snippet of HTML/JavaScript that you paste into your website's HTML where you want the form to appear. For GitHub Pages or any static HTML site, paste the snippet directly into the relevant section of your page. For Blogger, use an HTML/JavaScript widget. The form submits directly to Sender and adds the subscriber to your chosen list automatically.

Can I redirect subscribers to a custom thank you page after signup?

Yes. In the form settings within Sender, there is an option to specify a redirect URL after successful submission. We use this to send DRS subscribers to a branded thank you page on the main site, and WOS subscribers to a separate WOS-specific thank you page. It improves the post-signup experience and gives you a clean page to track conversions in Google Analytics.

Does Sender support double opt-in?

Yes. Double opt-in (where subscribers receive a confirmation email before being added to your list) can be enabled per form. For UK audiences and GDPR compliance, double opt-in is generally recommended — it confirms genuine consent and keeps your list clean. The confirmation email is customisable within the platform.

Deliverability and Practical Use

How good is Sender's deliverability?

Sender has solid deliverability for a free-tier platform. Emails reliably reach the inbox in our testing rather than defaulting to spam folders. For best results, avoid spam trigger words in subject lines, keep your list clean by removing bounces, and ensure you are sending from a verified sender address. On the free plan you send from a Sender-managed domain, which is generally trustworthy.

Can I manage multiple brands on one Sender account?

Within a single account you can create multiple separate subscriber lists and use different sender names and reply-to addresses per campaign. However, for fully separate brands we chose to use separate Sender accounts — one for DRS and one for WOS — to keep audience data, automation flows and branding completely clean between projects. There is no cost implication for having multiple free accounts provided each stays within the free limits.
