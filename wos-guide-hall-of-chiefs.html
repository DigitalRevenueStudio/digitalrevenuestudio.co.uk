👑 Early Game Event

Hall of Chiefs:
Complete F2P
Event Guide

Hall of Chiefs is one of the most important early events in WOS. It runs 3 times in Generation 1 and rewards you for doing the right things in the right order. Most players leave significant points on the table by spending on the wrong day.

Gen 1 and Gen 2 F2P Strategy ✓ Updated April 2026
⚡ Quick Summary
Runs In
Gen 1 (3x, 6 days each) and Gen 2 (7 days)
Core Rule

F2P Target
Hit all 4 daily milestones every day. Not top 10 overall.
Biggest Mistake
Spending speed-ups the week before the event opens
What Is Hall of Chiefs

The Event Context

⏰ When It Runs

Exclusive to Generation 1 and Generation 2 states. Runs 3 times in Gen 1 (6 days each). In Gen 2 it runs 7 days and the featured hero switches from Jeronimo to Philly. After Gen 2 it is replaced by King of Icefield.

🏆 Why It Matters

The top 10 players overall win Jeronimo shards. Even outside the top 10, milestone rewards include Gems, Speed-Ups, Mythic Skill Manuals and resources that meaningfully accelerate early progression.

⚡ The Core Rule

Each day has a specific scoring category. Spending resources on the wrong day scores nothing for HoC. Hoard everything in advance and spend it only on the day that rewards it.

📊 F2P Reality Check

Top 10 overall requires significant spending. Focus on hitting all 4 daily milestone thresholds every day instead. Those rewards compound and are fully achievable without spending real money.

The single most important HoC habit: Wait for President skills to activate before spending anything each day. President skills boost the points value of every action. Spending before they activate wastes the multiplier on resources you cannot recover.

The pre-event hoard: In the weeks before HoC, stop using speed-ups, hero shards and upgrade materials. Every speed-up burned casually the week before is a speed-up that scores no points during the event. The players who rank highest as F2P are almost always the ones who saved the longest.

Common Mistakes

What Most Players Get Wrong

Spending speed-ups casually in the weeks before HoC opens. This is the single most costly HoC mistake. Every construction or research speed-up used outside the event is a speed-up that earns no HoC points. Start hoarding 2 to 3 weeks before the event.

Using speed-ups on the wrong day. Research speed-ups used on Day 1 earn zero HoC points. Know the day-by-day scoring schedule before the event opens. Ask your R4/R5 to confirm the cycle for your specific HoC.

Not waiting for President skills. President skills multiply the points value of every resource you spend. Spending before they activate wastes the most impactful multiplier available each day.

Spending Gold Hero Shards before Day 6. Gold Hero Shards have significantly higher point values than standard shards. Holding them for the final day maximises their scoring contribution when the widest range of categories is active.

Chasing top 10 overall as F2P. Top 10 overall requires significant spending on Lucky Wheel spins and hero shards. Competing for it as F2P burns resources on a race you cannot win. Focus on hitting all 4 daily milestone thresholds instead. Those rewards are substantial and fully achievable.
