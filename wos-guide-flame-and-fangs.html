Flame and Fangs:
Complete F2P Guide

Flame and Fangs runs alongside State of Power and SvS every two weeks. If you are just doing your daily Intel Missions without a plan, you are leaving Fire Crystals and speedups on the table. This guide covers how it works, how to maximise Crystallite Cores and what to spend them on.

How It Works

What Is Flame and Fangs?

Flame and Fangs is a limited-time PvE event that activates when a State of Power or State vs State event begins on your server. It runs for two days, during which Intel Missions at the Lighthouse are modified to include Firebeasts and Firehunters. Defeating these earns Crystallite Cores, which you spend in the event shop for rewards.

Intel Missions refresh three times per day at 00:00, 08:00 and 16:00 server time. Each refresh gives 8 new Intel Missions and 2 new Firebeast missions. Completing everything at each refresh earns 28 Crystallite Cores per session. Across all 6 sessions over the two-day event, the standard maximum is 168 Crystallite Cores.

Note on Bounty Missions: Crystallite Cores are not earned from Bounty Intel Missions. Only standard Intel Missions and Firehunter missions contribute to your core total. Do not waste time on Bounties during this event.

Core Breakdown

Cores Per Session

Each Intel Mission refresh during the event gives you 8 standard missions at 1 core each, and 2 Firehunter missions at 10 cores each. That is 28 cores per refresh, 84 per day, 168 total across both event days.

Firehunters are the priority. Each Firehunter mission gives 10 cores compared to 1 core for a standard mission. Complete Firehunter missions first at every refresh to secure your highest-value cores before anything disrupts your session.

💡 Bonus Cores Trick: 168 to 184

The Sunday night before the event starts, complete your 08:00 UTC Intel Mission refresh as normal but do not collect the rewards. Leave them unclaimed overnight.

When the event begins at 00:00 UTC on Monday, collect those held missions. Because the event is now active, they count as event missions and award Crystallite Cores. You also receive the standard new Monday 00:00 refresh on top of this. The result is an extra 16 Crystallite Cores above the standard maximum.

This is a straightforward trick that requires only one habit change: remember not to claim your Sunday 08:00 missions before going to sleep.

💬 Community tip. Verify timings against your server time before the event.

Last resort

Do not let cores expire. Unspent Crystallite Cores convert to 10K Meat each when the event ends. Meat is one of the easiest resources to gather passively. Even spending leftover cores on resources is better than letting them expire. Always spend high-priority items first.

Refined Fire Crystals require FC6. If your state has not unlocked Fire Crystal level 6, Refined Fire Crystals will not appear in your shop. Redirect those cores to Fire Crystal x5 packs and speedups instead.

Common Mistakes

What Loses You Cores and Value

Missing refreshes. Each missed refresh costs you 28 Crystallite Cores. Over two days of the event, missing two refreshes is the difference between clearing your top rewards or falling short. Set a reminder for 00:00 and 08:00 server time.

Completing Bounty missions instead of standard Intel Missions. Bounty missions do not award Crystallite Cores during Flame and Fangs. Every session you spend on Bounties instead of standard Intel Missions is cores left on the table.

Spending on Hero EXP or resources before Fire Crystals. 10K Hero EXP costs 3 cores and has minimal progression impact. Fire Crystal x5 packs cost 5 cores and directly advance your Furnace milestones. The priority order in the reward table above reflects actual long-term value.

Forgetting to hold Sunday 08:00 missions. The bonus cores trick requires just one thing: not collecting your Sunday 08:00 refresh before the event starts. If you collect them out of habit, you lose 16 cores. A note or reminder the night before is enough to avoid this.

Letting cores expire. The event has a hard end. Cores that remain unspent convert to Meat. Even if you have already bought your priority items, spend remaining cores on speedups or resources rather than letting them expire.
