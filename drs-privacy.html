Privacy Policy

How Digital Revenue Studio collects, uses and protects your personal data.

Last updated: March 2026

Digital Revenue Studio is operated as an independent content studio based in the United Kingdom. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data in accordance with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

Who we are

Digital Revenue Studio (“we”, “us”, “our”) operates the website at digitalrevenuestudio.co.uk. If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at [email protected].

What data we collect

We only collect personal data that you voluntarily provide or that is gathered automatically when you visit our site:

  • Email address — when you sign up for our newsletter via the signup form on this site.
  • First name — optionally provided when you sign up, used to personalise emails.
  • Usage data — collected automatically via Google Analytics 4, including pages visited, time on site, device type and approximate location. This data is aggregated and anonymised.

How we use your data

  • Email address and name — used solely to send you our newsletter and studio updates via Sender (our email platform). We will never sell or share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Analytics data — used to understand how our site is used so we can improve it. We use Google Analytics 4 with standard settings. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking via the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Legal basis for processing

Under UK GDPR, our legal basis for processing your data is:

  • Consent — for email newsletter sign-ups. You can withdraw your consent at any time by unsubscribing via the link in any email we send.
  • Legitimate interests — for analytics data, to understand and improve our website.

Email marketing

If you sign up to our newsletter, your email address is stored and processed by Sender (sender.net), our email marketing platform. Sender is GDPR-compliant. You can unsubscribe at any time using the unsubscribe link included in every email. Once unsubscribed, we will not contact you again.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies set by Google Analytics to collect usage data. These are analytics cookies and do not contain any personally identifiable information. By continuing to use our site, you consent to these cookies being placed on your device.

Third-party links

Our site contains links to third-party websites including Amazon, Payhip, Sender and others. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies before providing any personal data.

Affiliate links

Some links on this site are affiliate links. Clicking these links and making a purchase may result in us earning a commission. We do not share any of your personal data with affiliate partners. Any tracking is handled entirely by the third-party platform and is subject to their own privacy policy.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at [email protected].

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed to our newsletter. Upon unsubscribing, your data is removed from our active mailing list. Analytics data is retained by Google in accordance with their data retention settings.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
